KINGA BORA

This website offers parents a digital platform to effortlessly monitor their children's vaccination records. It provides timely reminders for upcoming vaccinations, stores immunization history securely, and promotes proactive healthcare management for children.

Features

  • Securely store your child's vaccination records
  • Receive timely reminders for upcoming vaccinations
  • Access your child's immunization history anytime, anywhere
  • Monitor your child's vaccination progress
  • Get expert advice on vaccination schedules
